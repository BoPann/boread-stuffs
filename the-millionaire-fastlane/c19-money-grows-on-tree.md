# Does Money Grow on Trees? 
Short answer: Yes, if you own the money trees.

## Money Tree 
Money Trees are business system that can grow on its own. Here are 5 best seedings for that money trees. (in that order)

1. Rental Systems
	- real estate
	- petents
	- hotel 
	- storage
2. Computer/Software Systems
3. Content Systems
	- books 
	- blogs
	- social media
4. Distribution Systems 
	- Amazon sells products online
	- Startbucks sells coffee through franchizing
5. Human Resource Systems
	- Risky and low passivity. Good employee nurture money trees. Bad employee pluck the fruits of meowney trees and require pruning.
